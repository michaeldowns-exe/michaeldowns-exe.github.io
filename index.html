About Us

Crew for a Cause is a non-profit organization that brings the power of music to communities in need. Based in Orlando, our team of skilled DJs host events at schools and charity functions, using the uplifting energy of music to bring people together and support those in need. Our mission is to use the art of DJing to bring people together and make a positive impact on local communities. From school dances to charity events, we strive to create a safe and inclusive space for everyone to enjoy the magic of music. We believe that music has the power to heal, bring joy, and unite people from all walks of life. By volunteering our time and skills, we hope to make a lasting impact on the communities we serve. Join us in our mission to bring the power of music to those who need it most. Support our Sound Crew and help us make a difference, one beat at a time.

Michael Downs

Founder

Also known as DJ MDZ, this project started as a pure passion for mixing music. As I got involved with University High School's Student Government, I realized the positive power of music not only at my school but the community as a whole. Sound Crew is rooted in music as a medium to bring people together by crafting unforgettable events.
